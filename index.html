About DD HERTY

DD HERTY is a Ghanaian women-led company transforming organic hibiscus, shea butter, into premium skincare and beverages. We partner directly with 200+ rural women farmers to provide fair income while promoting sustainable, climate-smart farming. Our mission: Bring the power of African botanicals to the world.
